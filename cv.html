Current position

Junior Group Leader (Tenure Track) — group Aquatic Biogeochemistry of Carbon (ABC Lab), Leibniz-Institute of Freshwater Ecology and Inland Fisheries (IGB), Department 1 (Ecohydrology & Biogeochemistry); Programme Area 4. Since January 2026. Research programme: the ecology of molecules — dissolved organic matter (DOM) at molecular resolution (FT-ICR-MS) to predict and manage carbon-climate feedbacks and water quality.
